Top 10 Billionaires in the world


1. Elon R Musk

Elon Reeve musk (Nationality:US,Canada,South Africa) is a buisness magnate , entrepreneur and engineer. He is one of the wealthiest persons in the world with the net worth around US $209 billion. He is the founder , CEO and CTO of the private space company SpaceX and the CEO and product architect of the company Tesla,Inc. Tesla is one of the greatest electric autonomous car company in the world with a net worth around $650 billion. Tesla cars are quite popular among large number of peoples around the globe and lot of customers are attracted by the technology they offer. When talking about SpaceX it is a american space exploration and aerospace manufacturing private company with the goal to colonise Mars. OpenAI the largest AI software company is also lead under Elon Musk and he is the co-founder of the company. Musk is such a person among the most influenced peoples in the world and one of the powerful person among the Worlds most powerful people.

2. Jeff Bezos

Jeff Bezos(Nationality:US) is a industrialist , internet entrepreneur and inventor and he is well known to be the founder and CEO of one of the largest online E-commerce company Amazon. The worth of amazon is estimated as $1 trillion which leads Bezos to become one of the richest person in the world with a net worth around US $200 billion. Amazon was founded in late 1994 and started as a online book store which sells books of every title. Later the company is expanded and began to sell each and every products available across the world through the amazon platform. It was a great invention which ease the availability of any types of products manufactured in the entire world. In addition Bezos started another idea termed as Blue Origin which is a private aerospace manufacturing and spaceflight company for space tourism.

3. Bill Gates

William Henry Bill Gates III (Nationality, US) is an American business magnate, philanthropist, and was the chairman of Microsoft, the software company he founded with his child hood friend Paul Allen. He is ranked among the world's wealthiest people and was the wealthiest overall from 1995 to 2009, excluding 2008, when he was ranked third. He has also authored several books.Gates is one of the best-known entrepreneurs of the personal computer revolution. Microsoft offered system softwares which increased the popularity of personal computers. The PC manufacturing companies began approching him for the software he made. And the first operating system of Microsoft is named itself as Microsoft operating systems. Later the versions of this OS is named as Windows. He stepped down as chief executive officer of Microsoft in January 2000. He remained as chairman and the position of chief software architect. In June 2006 Gates announced that he would be transitioning from full-time work at Microsoft to part-time work, and full-time work at the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation. Currently he is the third richest person in the world with the worth of US $132 billion.

4. Bernard Arnault

Bernard Arnault (Nationality , French) is a businessman , art collector and media proprietor. He is well known to be the chairman and chief executive of LVMH Moët Hennessy Louis Vuitton, the world's largest luxury-goods company. He became the richest person in fashion and luxury in 2018. Arnault briefly surpassed Jeff Bezos to become the richest person in the world in December 2019. He is still considered as one of the wealthiest person in the world standing at the fourth position among the worlds Billionaires. In 2021 his net worth is approximately US $155 billion.

5. Mark Zuckerberg

Mark Elliot Zuckerberg (Nationality:US) is an American Internet entrepreneur and Computer programmer. He is best known for creating the social networking site Facebook, of which he is chief executive and president. It was co-founded as a private company by Zuckerberg and classmates Dustin Moskovitz, Eduardo Saverin, and Chris Hughes while they were students at Harvard University. In 2010, Zuckerberg was named Time magazine's Person of the Year. In 2012 Facebook acquired another online networking platform Instagram for $1 billion and Whatsapp in 2014 for $19 billion. The immense popularity of Facebook leads Zuckerberg to become one of the richest young individual in the world with the current net worth around $96 billion.

6. Warren Buffett

Warren E. Buffett, the CEO and Chairman of Berkshire Hathaway an American multinational conglomerate holding company. Buffet is well defined as the king of stock market, He became a millionaire in today’s dollars by age of 25 itself. He then moved back to his hometown of Omaha where he founded seven investment partnerships that returned an average annual rate of 30%. His investment ideas is always being an inspiration for those who have to head into stock market. After closing his partnerships, Buffett built Berkshire Hathaway into an empire with Charlie Munger, his Vice Chairman. By 2020 his worth is increased upto $85 billion and in 2021 his worth is estimated as $87.6 billion.

It also produces customer relationship management (CRM) and enterprise resource planning (ERP) softwares. The Oracle database softwares could able to compete with other softwares like Microsoft SQL server and Sybase, which is some of the popular database tools.
