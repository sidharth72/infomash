1. Dark Matter

Dark matter is a form of matter which allows galaxies to exist. These weird type matter don't produce , reflect and absorbe light and never interact with the Electromagnetic spectrum. So it cannot be detected by observing the electromagnetic radiation. Dark matter accounts for approximately 85% of the matter in the universe and about a quarter of its total massenergy density. The existance of dark matter is implied by it gravitational effects that cannot be explained by accepted theories of gravity. According to Relativity theory , Gravity is not actually a force but the curvature of spacetime by the effect of massive objects.
